# Basic Calculator

You are tasked with implementing a basic calculator that can perform four arithmetic operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. The calculator should take two numbers (float) as input and an operation symbol to specify which operation to perform. The output should be printed in two decimal places.

---

### Sample Output 1

```text
Enter the first number: 5
Enter the second number: 3
Enter the operation (+, -, *, /): /
Result: 1.67

### Sample Output 2

```text
Enter the first number: 10
Enter the second number: 4
Enter the operation (+, -, *, /): -
Result: 6.00

Enter the first number: 5.5
